Wondering what is Vidyalaya Mail? What can you do with it? Read on.

Professional email id

Email id is the key component of personal branding. With regular mail services, it is very difficult to get good email ids, as all good ones are already taken. So most of the students settle with ids like prabhu1987.rocks@gmail.com or prabhu_the_cute@yahoo.com, which are ugly and considered unprofessional.

With Vidyalaya Mail, you will get email id like prabhu@rmv.ac.in. Clean and Professional. Will create a positive impression when used in Resume or technical papers.

No expiration

With regular email services, your account will be deleted after 3-6 months of inactivity. With Vidyalaya Mail, your account stays forever, whether you use it or not. You can continue using it even after your graduation. You are one of us, whether as a student or as an alumni.

No Advertisements

Vidyalaya Mail service is 100% ad-free. You will never see any kind of ads anywhere in the whole suite. Period. Welcome to distraction-less online experience.

Address Auto-complete

Are you trying to reach your classmates or teachers, but don't know their email id? No problem, just pull up compose page and start typing their name or register number. Their email id will popup automatically.

Forgot your password?

With regular mail services, if you forget your password, it's a pain to reset it. You have to answer so many questions and you don't even remember what answers you gave when you registered. With Vidyalaya Mail, just send an email to help@rmv.ac.in and we will reset it within minutes.

Account Hacked?

Ever thought about it? What would you do when somebody hacks into your gmail or yahoo account? What if they change security questions? Literally you are helpless. Help desk can not help you because they can not distinguish between you and hacker.

With Vidyalaya Mail, it's dead simple. We know you personally, so if you say you account is hacked, we will restore and give it back to you. No questions asked.

Simple Export/Import

You can import all your existing emails from gmail or yahoo to Vidyalaya Mail or export/forward Vidyalaya Mails to your personal account. With a simple one-time setup, you can eliminate the need to check multiple accounts and save yourself a lot of time.

Groups
